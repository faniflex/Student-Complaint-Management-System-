SIMPLE PROCESS

How It Works in 4 Easy Steps

From submission to resolution, our streamlined process ensures efficiency and transparency at every stage.

1

Submit Complaint

Students log in and submit complaints through our user-friendly form with category selection and evidence upload.

2

Automated Routing

System automatically routes complaints to the appropriate department based on category and priority level.

3

Review & Action

Assigned staff members review complaints, conduct investigations, and implement appropriate solutions.

4

Resolution & Feedback

Students receive updates and final resolution. The system collects feedback for continuous improvement.
